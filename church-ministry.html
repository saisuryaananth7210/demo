New Life and Light Ministry

NLLM organization was started in 1990 by Bishop Victor Pierson, his wife Roslin and his son Johnson Pradeep with god's grace.

Our mission is to spread the knowledge of the Bible, the holy book that guides and enriches the lives of millions around the world. Through our teachings, we hope to inspire individuals to find meaning, purpose, and spiritual fulfillment in their lives, and to live according to the principles of love, compassion, and justice.

We believe that the Bible offers a path to a better world, and we are committed to sharing this message with as many people as possible.

The New Life and Light Assembly Church is dedicated to the furtherance of the Gospel, equipping believers, evangelizing the lost, and proclaiming the inerrant biblical account of history. We believe that every person is a beloved child of God, and we strive to create an inclusive and diverse community where everyone feels accepted and valued.

We welcome individuals of all ages, backgrounds, and beliefs, and invite you to come as you are and discover the grace and love of God that is available to all.
