Mission

Since the 2006, New Life and Light Foundation have been one of the world’s leading charity organizations in ending poverty. Our NLLM mission continues to be to provide aid all over the world to communities and families in poverty.

Our mission is to make a positive impact in the lives of those in need, and we rely on the generosity of individuals and organizations like you to make it happen.

Our Values

Our New Life and Light Trust actions are guided by Christian principles:

  • 1. Love: We believe in Christ's commandment to love one another, and we put love into action through our service.
  • 2. Compassion: We empathize with those who are suffering and seek to alleviate their pain and distress.
  • 3. Empowerment: We empower individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty and dependency.
  • 4. Integrity: We operate with transparency, accountability, and integrity in all our endeavors.
  • 5. Inclusivity: We serve people of all backgrounds, races, and religions, reflecting Christ's all-encompassing love.

What we do

We New Life and Light Charitable Trust or Organization, we are dedicated to a variety of causes, ranging from providing food and shelter to the homeless, to supporting education and healthcare in underprivileged communities.

We work tirelessly to ensure that every donation we receive is maximized to its full potential, creating a lasting impact in the lives of those we serve.

  • Scholarships to poor children
  • Marriage for economically weaker sections of society
  • Free medical camps and blood donation camps in different parts of the country, Conducting Free Eye Camps in villages.
  • Provide books, stationary and other educational accessories to the poor.
  • Creating awareness among the public about the road safety norms
  • Help widows and old aged people.
  • Awareness campaigns to protect the environment, Promoting Rainwater Harvesting.
  • Provide clean drinking water to residents of the remote villages.
  • Awareness Camps about health and hygiene are contacted in villages.
  • Give financial support to similar charitable organizations.
  • Provide relief for the poor and help incapacitate persons.

How You Can Help

There are several ways you can get involved and make a difference:

  • Donate: Your contributions are invaluable in sustaining our programs and initiatives. Every dollar you donate goes directly towards supporting those in need. Whether it's a one-time donation or a recurring monthly contribution, your generosity can change lives.
  • Volunteer: Join our team of dedicated volunteers and be a part of something meaningful. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or make a long-term commitment, your time and skills can bring hope and happiness to those who need it the most.
  • Spread the Word: Help us raise awareness about our cause by sharing our website (nllm.in) New Life and Light Mission, social media posts, and events with your family, friends, and colleagues. Together, we can reach a wider audience, spark conversations, and inspire more people to take action.
